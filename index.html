Minecraft to Roblox Skin Converter

How to use:

  1. Click "Choose File" to upload your Minecraft skin (.png file)
  2. The tool will automatically convert it to Roblox shirt and pants templates
  3. Download the templates and upload them to Roblox

Note: This tool preserves the original pixel art style by using nearest neighbor scaling.
